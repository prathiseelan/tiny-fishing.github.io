How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to brake or drift.
  • Customize and upgrade your car in the garage to improve its performance.
  • Compete in races and championships to earn rewards and unlock new tracks and cars.

Welcome to Wheely 8

Embark on a brand new adventure with Wheely in Wheely 8, the latest installment of the popular puzzle series. Join Wheely as he travels through time and space to rescue his beloved girlfriend, Jolie, who has been kidnapped by a mysterious villain. With its captivating storyline, challenging puzzles, and charming characters, Wheely 8 promises an unforgettable journey that will captivate players of all ages.

In this thrilling chapter of the series, Wheely must navigate through various time periods and dimensions, from ancient Egypt to futuristic worlds, in his quest to save Jolie. Help Wheely overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and outwit clever enemies as he races against time to rescue his true love.

Wheely 8 features a wide variety of puzzles and challenges set in different time periods and locations. From deciphering ancient hieroglyphs to hacking high-tech security systems, Wheely 8 offers an exciting and diverse gameplay experience that will test players' problem-solving skills and creativity.

With its vibrant graphics, intuitive controls, and delightful storyline, Wheely 8 is suitable for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a casual gamer looking for a fun and entertaining experience, Wheely 8 offers something for everyone to enjoy.

Key Features

  • Exciting storyline set across various time periods and dimensions.
  • Diverse puzzles and challenges inspired by different historical and futuristic settings.
  • Quirky characters and humorous dialogue that bring the adventure to life.
  • Hours of fun and entertainment for players of all ages.
  • Beautifully designed levels with vibrant visuals and immersive environments.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to interact with objects and navigate Wheely through each level.
  • Click on items in the environment to trigger actions and solve puzzles.
  • Help Wheely overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and rescue Jolie from the clutches of the villain.
  • Collect stars and unlock bonus levels for additional challenges and rewards.

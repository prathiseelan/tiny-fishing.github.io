How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to jump over obstacles and slide under hazards.
  • Avoid traps and hazards while collecting power-ups to enhance your abilities.
  • Race against the clock to reach the finish line before time runs out.

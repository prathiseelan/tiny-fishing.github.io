Welcome to Wheely 5

Prepare for another thrilling adventure with Wheely in Wheely 5, the latest installment of the popular puzzle series. Join Wheely as he embarks on a quest to rescue his beloved girlfriend from the clutches of an evil car thief. With its captivating storyline, challenging puzzles, and charming characters, Wheely 5 offers an unforgettable gaming experience that will keep you entertained for hours.

In this exciting chapter of the series, Wheely must travel through a variety of colorful and imaginative landscapes, from bustling city streets to treacherous jungles, in search of his missing girlfriend. Help Wheely navigate through each level by solving a series of increasingly difficult puzzles, overcoming obstacles, and outwitting cunning enemies. With each new challenge, Wheely gets closer to rescuing his girlfriend and foiling the villain's evil plans.

Wheely 5 features a wide range of puzzles and challenges, each designed to test your logic, problem-solving skills, and creativity. From navigating complex mazes to interacting with quirky characters, Wheely 5 offers a diverse and engaging gameplay experience that will keep you coming back for more.

With its vibrant graphics, intuitive controls, and delightful storyline, Wheely 5 is suitable for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a casual gamer looking for a fun and relaxing experience, Wheely 5 offers something for everyone to enjoy.

Key Features

  • Exciting storyline filled with adventure and suspense.
  • Dozens of challenging puzzles and obstacles to overcome.
  • Colorful and imaginative landscapes to explore.
  • Quirky characters and humorous dialogue to enjoy along the way.
  • Hours of fun and entertainment for players of all ages.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to interact with objects and navigate Wheely through each level.
  • Click on items in the environment to trigger actions and solve puzzles.
  • Help Wheely overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and progress through each level.
  • Collect stars and unlock bonus levels for additional challenges and rewards.

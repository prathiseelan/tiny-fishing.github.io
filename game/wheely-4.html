Welcome to Wheely 4

Embark on a new adventure with Wheely in Wheely 4, the latest installment of the popular puzzle series. Join Wheely as he sets off on a quest to explore distant lands, solve challenging puzzles, and uncover hidden secrets. With its captivating storyline, charming characters, and clever puzzles, Wheely 4 offers an unforgettable gaming experience that will keep you entertained for hours.

In this exciting chapter of the series, Wheely finds himself on a journey to a far-off land filled with mysterious temples, ancient ruins, and treacherous traps. Help Wheely navigate through each level by solving a variety of puzzles, overcoming obstacles, and outsmarting cunning enemies. With each new challenge, Wheely gets closer to uncovering the secrets of this mysterious land and unlocking its hidden treasures.

Wheely 4 features a wide range of puzzles and challenges, each designed to test your logic, creativity, and problem-solving skills. From navigating perilous landscapes to interacting with quirky characters, Wheely 4 offers a diverse and engaging gameplay experience that will keep you coming back for more.

With its colorful graphics, intuitive controls, and whimsical charm, Wheely 4 is suitable for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a casual gamer looking for a fun and relaxing experience, Wheely 4 offers something for everyone to enjoy.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to interact with objects and navigate Wheely through each level.
  • Click on items in the environment to trigger actions and solve puzzles.
  • Help Wheely overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and progress through each level.
  • Collect stars and unlock bonus levels for additional challenges and rewards.

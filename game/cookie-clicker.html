Welcome to Cookie Clicker

  • Indulge your sweet tooth and embark on a delectable journey in Cookie Clicker, the addictive incremental game where you bake cookies to your heart's content. Click, upgrade, and expand your cookie empire as you strive to become the ultimate cookie tycoon.
  • Experience the satisfying sensation of clicking your way to cookie greatness as you bake batches of delicious treats. Start with a humble oven and a few cookies, then watch as your bakery grows into a sprawling empire filled with mouthwatering delights.
  • What sets Cookie Clicker apart is its addictive gameplay loop and endless possibilities for cookie creation. With an array of upgrades, achievements, and special cookies to unlock, there's always something new to discover as you strive to bake the perfect batch.
  • Challenge yourself to bake as many cookies as possible in the single-player mode, or compete against friends and players from around the world in multiplayer cookie-baking competitions. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Cookie Clicker is perfect for players of all ages.
  • Immerse yourself in the deliciously addictive world of Cookie Clicker and satisfy your craving for cookies. Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick treat or a dedicated baker aiming for cookie domination, this game has something for everyone.

How to Play

  • Click on the giant cookie to bake cookies and earn currency.
  • Use your currency to purchase upgrades, buildings, and special cookies to increase your cookie production.
  • Expand your bakery empire by unlocking new buildings and upgrading existing ones.
  • Keep clicking and baking to reach new milestones and unlock achievements.
  • Compete with friends and players worldwide to see who can bake the most cookies!

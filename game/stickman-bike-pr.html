Welcome to Stickman Bike PR

  • Race through exhilarating tracks and perform jaw-dropping stunts in Stickman Bike PR, an adrenaline-fueled biking game that will push your skills to the limit. Get ready to defy gravity and become the ultimate stickman biker!
  • Experience the thrill of high-speed biking as you navigate through challenging courses filled with obstacles and ramps. With its intuitive controls and realistic physics, Stickman Bike PR offers a truly immersive biking experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your bike.
  • Balance your bike to avoid crashing and maintain control on challenging terrain.
  • Perform stunts by pressing designated keys while in the air.
  • Complete each track as quickly as possible to earn stars and unlock new content.

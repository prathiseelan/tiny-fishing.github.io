Welcome to Retro Highway

  • Get ready for a nostalgic journey through the golden age of arcade racing with Retro Highway, a thrilling motorcycle racing game that pays homage to classic 8-bit racing games. Strap on your helmet, rev up your engine, and hit the open road as you race to the finish line!
  • Experience the adrenaline rush of high-speed motorcycle racing as you navigate through retro-inspired pixelated environments. Dodge traffic, perform epic stunts, and collect coins as you race through bustling city streets and scenic countryside roads.
  • What sets Retro Highway apart is its charming retro graphics, intuitive controls, and addictive gameplay. With simple one-touch controls, you'll be weaving through traffic and pulling off daring maneuvers in no time.
  • Challenge yourself to complete a variety of missions and objectives as you progress through the game. From reaching top speeds to performing flawless stunts, there's always a new challenge waiting around the next corner.
  • Immerse yourself in the nostalgia of classic arcade racing and relive the excitement of the arcade era with Retro Highway. Are you ready to burn rubber and become the ultimate retro racer?

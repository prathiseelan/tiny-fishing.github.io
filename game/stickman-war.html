Welcome to Stickman War

  • Prepare for an epic battle in Stickman War, where you'll lead a fearless stickman army into intense combat against enemy forces. Engage in adrenaline-pumping warfare across various battlegrounds and prove your tactical prowess!
  • Experience the thrill of strategic warfare as you command your stickman troops in gripping battles. Navigate through treacherous terrain, deploy your units strategically, and unleash devastating attacks to crush your opponents.
  • What sets Stickman War apart is its dynamic gameplay and vast array of battle scenarios. From skirmishes in dense forests to urban warfare in bustling cities, each level presents unique challenges and opportunities for victory.
  • Challenge yourself to outmaneuver and outsmart your enemies in thrilling multiplayer battles. Form alliances, coordinate attacks, and lead your army to glory in action-packed showdowns against other players from around the world.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to navigate menus and select units.
  • Deploy your stickman troops strategically on the battlefield.
  • Command your units to attack enemy forces and defend your territory.
  • Upgrade your army and unlock new abilities to gain the upper hand in battle.

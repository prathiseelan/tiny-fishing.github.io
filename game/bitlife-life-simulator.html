Welcome to BitLife - Life Simulator

  • Step into the captivating world of BitLife - Life Simulator, where you can live out an entire virtual life from birth to death. In this immersive simulation game, you'll navigate through the complexities of life, making crucial decisions and experiencing the consequences of your actions.
  • Start by customizing your character and choosing your starting attributes. From there, you'll embark on a journey filled with endless possibilities. Will you focus on academics and strive for a successful career, or will you indulge in a life of crime and debauchery? The choice is yours.
  • Explore a vast array of careers, relationships, and life experiences as you progress through different stages of life. From childhood to old age, every decision you make will shape your character's destiny and determine their ultimate legacy.
  • What sets BitLife apart is its realistic portrayal of everyday life, complete with unexpected twists and turns. Navigate through challenges such as relationships, finances, and health issues, and see how your choices impact your character's well-being and happiness.
  • Challenge yourself to achieve various milestones and accomplishments, from graduating college to becoming a famous celebrity. Along the way, you'll encounter a diverse cast of characters and encounter random events that will test your resilience and adaptability.

How to Play

  • Create your character and customize their appearance and attributes.
  • Make decisions that will shape your character's life, including their career, relationships, and personal development.
  • Manage your character's finances, health, and happiness to ensure their well-being and success.
  • Explore different career paths, hobbies, and life experiences to unlock new opportunities and challenges.
  • Experience the consequences of your actions as you navigate through the ups and downs of life.

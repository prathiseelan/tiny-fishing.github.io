How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle's movement.
  • Press the spacebar or other designated keys to accelerate, brake, and use weapons.
  • Collect power-ups and weapons scattered throughout the track to gain an advantage over your opponents.

  • Master the tracks and outmaneuver your rivals to cross the finish line first and claim victory!

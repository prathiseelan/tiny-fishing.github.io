Welcome to Draw And Save Stickman

  • Unleash your creativity in Draw And Save Stickman, where you have the power to create and customize your own stickman character. Design your stickman, equip them with weapons and armor, and embark on epic adventures!
  • Experience the freedom of drawing your own stickman character and watching them come to life on the screen. With a wide range of customization options, including different colors, accessories, and weapons, you can create a stickman that reflects your unique style.
  • What sets Draw And Save Stickman apart is its open-world sandbox environment, where you can explore, battle enemies, and complete quests at your own pace. Whether you prefer action-packed battles or peaceful exploration, there's something for everyone in this game.
  • Challenge yourself to complete various missions and quests as you journey through the stickman world. Battle fierce enemies, solve puzzles, and uncover hidden treasures as you progress through the game.
  • Immerse yourself in the creative and adventurous world of Draw And Save Stickman and unleash your imagination. With its endless possibilities for customization and exploration, this game offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse or touchscreen to draw your stickman character.
  • Customize your stickman with different colors, accessories, and weapons.
  • Explore the world, interact with NPCs, and complete quests to progress through the game.
  • Battle enemies using your stickman's weapons and abilities.

Welcome to Ape Sling

  • Embark on a thrilling adventure with Ape Sling, a game that takes you into the heart of the jungle. Swing through the treetops as a nimble ape, navigating through lush environments filled with challenges and surprises.
  • Each level in Ape Sling presents unique obstacles and opportunities. Master the art of swinging and timing to propel your ape through the jungle canopy, collecting bananas and avoiding hazards along the way.
  • What sets Ape Sling apart is its dynamic gameplay, vibrant visuals, and intuitive controls. Experience the exhilaration of flinging your ape from vine to vine and unlocking new levels as you progress through the jungle.
  • Challenge yourself with increasing levels of difficulty, encounter quirky jungle creatures, and aim for high scores as you aim to become the top swinger in the jungle.
  • Immerse yourself in the energetic world of Ape Sling, where each swing brings you closer to the heart of the jungle and exciting new challenges.

How to Play

  • Click and hold to grab a vine.
  • Release to swing your ape to the next vine.
  • Collect bananas and power-ups to boost your score.
  • Avoid obstacles and navigate through challenging terrain.
  • Complete levels and aim for the highest scores in Ape Sling!

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to activate your turbo boost.
  • Drift around corners to maintain speed and gain an advantage over your opponents.
  • Collect coins and power-ups scattered across the track to improve your performance.

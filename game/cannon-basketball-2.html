Welcome to Cannon Basketball 2

  • Get ready for some high-flying basketball action in Cannon Basketball 2, the exciting sequel to the popular physics-based sports game. Step onto the court and show off your skills as you aim, shoot, and score baskets using a powerful cannon.
  • Experience the thrill of basketball like never before as you launch basketballs through challenging obstacles and into the hoop. With intuitive controls and addictive gameplay, Cannon Basketball 2 offers hours of fun for players of all ages.
  • What sets Cannon Basketball 2 apart is its clever level design and creative challenges. Navigate through intricate levels filled with moving platforms, switches, and other obstacles as you try to sink baskets from a variety of angles and distances.
  • Challenge yourself in the single-player mode, where you'll face off against increasingly difficult levels and obstacles. With multiple game modes and customizable cannons, Cannon Basketball 2 offers endless opportunities for creative gameplay and skillful shots.
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant graphics and upbeat soundtrack of Cannon Basketball 2, creating a lively and engaging gaming experience. With its addictive gameplay and challenging levels, Cannon Basketball 2 is sure to keep you entertained for hours on end.

How to Play

  • Adjust the angle and power of your cannon using the mouse or touchscreen.
  • Aim carefully and shoot the basketball into the hoop to score points.
  • Use the environment to your advantage, bouncing basketballs off walls and obstacles to reach difficult-to-reach baskets.
  • Complete each level by sinking all the baskets within the given number of shots.
  • Unlock new levels, cannons, and achievements as you progress through the game and improve your skills.

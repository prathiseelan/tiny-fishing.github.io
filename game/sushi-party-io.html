How to Play

  • Use the mouse to control your sushi character.
  • Navigate the conveyor belt and eat as much sushi as possible.
  • Avoid colliding with other players to maintain your speed and momentum.
  • Collect sushi pieces and power-ups to gain an advantage over your opponents.

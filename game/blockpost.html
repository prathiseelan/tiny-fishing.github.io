Welcome to Block Post

  • Step into the world of Block Post, an engaging puzzle game that challenges players to strategically place blocks to clear rows and earn points. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Block Post offers endless hours of fun and mental stimulation.
  • Explore a variety of game modes, each offering a unique twist on classic block puzzle gameplay. Whether you prefer a relaxed experience or a fast-paced challenge, Block Post has something for everyone.
  • What sets Block Post apart is its intuitive controls and dynamic level design. Players can easily drag and drop blocks onto the grid, making it easy to strategize and create satisfying combos.
  • Challenge yourself to beat your high score and climb the leaderboards. With each game session, you'll improve your skills and discover new strategies to maximize your points and clear more rows.
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant and colorful world of Block Post. With its charming visuals and catchy soundtrack, Block Post provides a relaxing and enjoyable gaming experience for players of all ages.

How to Play

  • Drag and drop blocks onto the grid to fill rows and clear them.
  • Strategically place blocks to create combos and earn bonus points.
  • Clear multiple rows at once to achieve higher scores.
  • Keep an eye on upcoming blocks and plan your moves accordingly.
  • Beat your high score and compete with friends for bragging rights.

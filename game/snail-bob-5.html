Welcome to Snail Bob 5

  • Join Snail Bob on another thrilling adventure in Snail Bob 5, the fifth installment of the popular puzzle game series. This time, Bob finds himself in ancient Egypt, where he must navigate treacherous obstacles and solve challenging puzzles to uncover hidden secrets!

  • Similar to previous games in the series, Snail Bob 5 features intuitive controls and clever level design. Use your mouse to interact with objects in each level, manipulating the environment to help Bob overcome obstacles and reach his destination.
  • Prepare to encounter new challenges and obstacles unique to the ancient Egyptian setting. From pyramids to mummies, each level presents Bob with puzzles that will test his problem-solving skills and your ability to strategize.

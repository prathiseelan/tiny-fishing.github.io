Welcome to Block Toggle

  • Enter the world of Block Toggle, a captivating puzzle game where players manipulate blocks to clear the board and solve challenging puzzles. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Block Toggle offers hours of fun and brain-teasing challenges.
  • Explore a variety of levels, each with its own unique layout and obstacles. From basic puzzles to complex brain teasers, Block Toggle offers a wide range of challenges suitable for players of all skill levels.
  • What sets Block Toggle apart is its intuitive controls and clever level design. Players can easily swipe to move blocks or tap to rotate them, making it easy to strategize and solve puzzles on the go.
  • Challenge yourself to complete each level in the fewest moves possible and earn three stars for your performance. With over a hundred levels to explore, Block Toggle offers endless hours of puzzle-solving fun.
  • Immerse yourself in the colorful and vibrant world of Block Toggle. With its charming graphics and catchy soundtrack, Block Toggle provides a delightful gaming experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to Play

  • Swipe to move blocks horizontally or vertically.
  • Tap to rotate blocks to fit into tight spaces or align with other blocks.
  • Clear the board by matching blocks of the same color.
  • Complete each level in the fewest moves possible to earn three stars.
  • Advance through increasingly challenging levels and unlock new puzzles.

Welcome to Xmas Rooftop Battles

Get into the holiday spirit with Xmas Rooftop Battles, a festive multiplayer game that combines the thrill of rooftop battles with the magic of Christmas. Join the fun as you engage in epic snowball fights against players from around the world in this winter wonderland adventure!

In Xmas Rooftop Battles, you'll find yourself atop snowy rooftops adorned with twinkling lights and festive decorations. Use your snowball-throwing skills to take down your opponents while dodging their attacks and maneuvering through the snowy terrain. With its fast-paced gameplay and intuitive controls, Xmas Rooftop Battles offers endless excitement for players of all ages.

Choose from a variety of characters, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles, and customize your appearance to stand out on the battlefield. Collect power-ups and special items scattered throughout the rooftops to gain an advantage over your opponents and secure victory in the ultimate holiday showdown.

Whether you're playing solo or teaming up with friends, Xmas Rooftop Battles offers a variety of game modes to suit your preferences. Battle it out in classic free-for-all matches, team up with friends in exciting team battles, or compete in special holiday events for a chance to earn exclusive rewards.

With its charming holiday-themed graphics, lively soundtrack, and festive atmosphere, Xmas Rooftop Battles is the perfect game to get you into the holiday spirit. So grab your snowballs, deck the halls, and prepare for an unforgettable Christmas adventure in Xmas Rooftop Battles!

Key Features

  • Festive multiplayer battles set atop snowy rooftops decorated for the holidays.
  • Fast-paced gameplay with intuitive controls for exciting snowball fights.
  • Customizable characters with unique abilities and appearance options.
  • A variety of game modes, including free-for-all matches and team battles.
  • Special holiday events and exclusive rewards to keep the fun going all season long.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to aim and throw snowballs at your opponents.
  • Move your character with the WASD keys or arrow keys to dodge incoming attacks.
  • Collect power-ups and special items to gain an advantage on the battlefield.
  • Work with your team to outmaneuver and outsmart your opponents in team battles.

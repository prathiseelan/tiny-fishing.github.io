Welcome to Foot Chinko

Foot Chinko is a captivating soccer-themed arcade game that combines elements of pinball and football. Immerse yourself in the fast-paced world of Foot Chinko as you guide your team to victory through a series of challenging matches and exhilarating gameplay!

In Foot Chinko, your objective is simple: score as many goals as possible while preventing your opponent from doing the same. Use the flippers at the bottom of the screen to launch the ball into the field and strategically bounce it off obstacles and players to outmaneuver the opposing team's defense.

With its intuitive controls and dynamic physics engine, Foot Chinko offers an addictive gaming experience that will keep you coming back for more. Master the art of timing and precision to execute skillful shots and passes, and lead your team to victory in every match.

But be prepared for a challenge, as the competition in Foot Chinko is fierce! As you progress through the game, you'll face increasingly skilled opponents and encounter new obstacles and power-ups that will test your skills and strategic prowess.

Whether you're playing against the computer or competing against friends in multiplayer mode, Foot Chinko offers endless hours of entertainment and excitement. So grab your flippers, hit the field, and experience the thrill of soccer like never before in Foot Chinko!

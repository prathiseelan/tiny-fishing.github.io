Welcome to Drunken Boxing 2

Get ready for an uproarious fighting experience in Drunken Boxing 2, where the laws of physics take a backseat and chaotic fun reigns supreme. Step into the shoes of a tipsy brawler and engage in hilarious bouts against equally inebriated opponents!

Embark on a wobbly journey as you navigate through a series of unconventional arenas, each more absurd than the last. From dimly lit alleys to swaying ships, every location presents its own set of challenges and comedic moments.

What sets Drunken Boxing 2 apart is its unique gameplay mechanics, where movement and attacks are influenced by the whimsical physics of inebriation. Swing wildly, stumble unpredictably, and deliver hilarious punches as you strive for victory!

Challenge friends or take on AI opponents in uproarious multiplayer battles that will leave everyone in stitches. With its blend of absurdity and competitive action, Drunken Boxing 2 promises endless laughter and unforgettable moments.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character.
  • Press the spacebar to punch and knock out your opponents.
  • Watch out for environmental hazards and obstacles that can trip you up!

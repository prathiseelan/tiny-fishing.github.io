Welcome to Bad Ice Cream 3

  • Chill out with the frosty fun of Bad Ice Cream 3. Enter a world of frozen treats and maze challenges as you control adorable ice cream characters on a quest to collect fruits in icy mazes.
  • Each level brings new challenges and puzzles. Navigate through intricate mazes, avoiding enemies and obstacles, and strategically use ice blocks to your advantage.
  • What sets Bad Ice Cream 3 apart is its delightful pixel art, quirky characters, and engaging multiplayer gameplay. Team up with friends or go solo in this frosty adventure filled with surprises and sweet rewards.
  • Challenge yourself through various levels with increasing difficulty, encountering new enemies and unlocking exciting power-ups along the way.
  • Immerse yourself in the addictively cool world of Bad Ice Cream 3. Whether you're a solo ice cream adventurer or part of a cooperative team, the frozen challenges await!

How to Play

  • Control your ice cream character using the arrow keys.
  • Navigate through mazes, collecting fruits and avoiding enemies.
  • Strategically use ice blocks to create barriers and protect yourself.
  • Team up with friends for multiplayer fun or go solo for a solo ice cream adventure.
  • Conquer levels, unlock power-ups, and enjoy the frosty challenges of Bad Ice Cream 3!

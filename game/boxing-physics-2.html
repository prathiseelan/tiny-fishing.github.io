How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your boxer's movement.
  • Press the spacebar or other designated keys to punch, block, or dodge.
  • Avoid getting hit by your opponent's punches while landing your own to score points.
  • Knock out your opponent by reducing their health bar to zero.
  • Enjoy the hilarity of the physics-based chaos and have fun!

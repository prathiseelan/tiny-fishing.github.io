Welcome to Snail Bob 4

  • Embark on another exciting adventure with Snail Bob in Snail Bob 4, the fourth installment of the popular puzzle game series. Join Bob as he sets out on a journey to rescue his grandpa from the clutches of an evil forest wizard!
  • Featuring charming graphics and addictive gameplay, Snail Bob 4 offers players a delightful experience filled with clever puzzles and exciting challenges. Help Bob navigate through a magical world filled with dangers and surprises.
  • Just like its predecessors, Snail Bob 4 boasts intuitive controls and clever level design. Use your mouse to interact with objects in each level, solving puzzles and avoiding obstacles to guide Bob safely to his destination.
  • Prepare to encounter new enemies and obstacles as you progress through the game, each presenting unique challenges that will test your problem-solving skills. Can you help Bob overcome the forest wizard's traps and rescue his grandpa?

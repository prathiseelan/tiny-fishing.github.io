Welcome to Bobblehead Soccer

  • Step onto the pitch and enter the whimsical world of Bobblehead Soccer, a delightful and fast-paced sports game where you control adorable bobblehead characters in exhilarating soccer matches.
  • Engage in thrilling one-on-one duels or exciting multiplayer tournaments as you dribble, pass, and shoot your way to victory. With its simple controls and intuitive gameplay, Bobblehead Soccer offers endless fun for players of all ages.
  • Choose your favorite bobblehead character from a diverse roster of quirky avatars, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. Whether you prefer speedy strikers, agile midfielders, or solid defenders, there's a bobblehead for every playing style.
  • What sets Bobblehead Soccer apart is its charming graphics and dynamic gameplay. Experience the excitement of scoring goals and pulling off jaw-dropping saves as you compete against friends or AI opponents in action-packed matches.
  • Immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere of Bobblehead Soccer with its vibrant stadiums and catchy soundtrack. With its addictive gameplay and competitive spirit, this game will keep you entertained for hours on end.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe gestures to move your bobblehead character.
  • Press the spacebar or tap the screen to kick the ball.
  • Defend your goal by positioning your character strategically and timing your tackles.
  • Score goals by dribbling past opponents and shooting the ball into the net.
  • Win matches to unlock new characters, stadiums, and game modes.

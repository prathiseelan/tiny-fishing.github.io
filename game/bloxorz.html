Welcome to Bloxorz

  • Enter the world of Bloxorz, a challenging puzzle game that will test your spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Bloxorz offers hours of brain-teasing fun for players of all ages.
  • Control the block-shaped protagonist as you navigate through a series of increasingly complex levels. Your objective is to maneuver the block to the designated goal tile without falling off the edge or getting trapped.
  • What sets Bloxorz apart is its clever level design and intuitive controls. Players can easily move the block forward, backward, and sideways using arrow keys or swipe gestures, making it easy to strategize and plan your moves.
  • Challenge yourself to beat each level in the fewest moves possible and earn a perfect score. With over a hundred levels to conquer, Bloxorz offers plenty of brain-teasing challenges to keep you entertained for hours on end.
  • Immerse yourself in the minimalist yet captivating world of Bloxorz. With its sleek graphics and ambient soundtrack, Bloxorz provides a relaxing and immersive gaming experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe gestures to move the block.
  • Avoid falling off the edge or getting trapped between tiles.
  • Maneuver the block to the designated goal tile to complete each level.
  • Strategize and plan your moves carefully to achieve a perfect score.
  • Advance through increasingly challenging levels and unlock new puzzles.

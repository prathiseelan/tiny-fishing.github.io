How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to control your car.
  • Drift around corners by tapping the handbrake and steering simultaneously.
  • Avoid obstacles and other racers while maintaining control of your vehicle.
  • Complete each race within the designated time limit to advance to the next level.
  • Upgrade your car's performance and appearance using the in-game currency earned from races.

Welcome to Stickman Dragon Fight

  • Prepare for an epic battle against ferocious dragons in Stickman Dragon Fight, where you'll embark on a quest to save the kingdom from the wrath of fire-breathing beasts. Arm yourself with courage and skill as you face off against these mythical creatures!
  • Experience the thrill of intense combat as you control a fearless stickman warrior on a mission to defeat the menacing dragons. With its fast-paced action and dynamic fighting mechanics, Stickman Dragon Fight delivers an adrenaline-pumping gaming experience.
  • What sets Stickman Dragon Fight apart is its variety of challenging levels and powerful enemies. From ancient ruins to fiery lairs, navigate diverse environments and confront increasingly formidable dragons as you progress through the game.
  • Challenge yourself to master different fighting styles and unlock special abilities to enhance your combat prowess. Customize your stickman warrior with unique weapons and armor, and unleash devastating attacks to vanquish your foes.
  • Immerse yourself in the epic world of Stickman Dragon Fight and engage in thrilling battles against epic bosses. Test your skills in intense one-on-one duels or team up with friends in multiplayer mode to take down the most fearsome dragons together!

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your stickman warrior.
  • Press the J, K, and L keys to perform various attacks and combos.
  • Defeat enemies and collect power-ups to upgrade your abilities.
  • Customize your stickman with different weapons and armor for battle.

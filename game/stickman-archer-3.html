Welcome to Stickman Archer 3

  • Enter the intense world of archery combat with Stickman Archer 3, where you'll face off against hordes of stickman enemies in epic battles of precision and skill. Take aim, draw your bow, and unleash deadly arrows to emerge victorious!
  • Prepare for adrenaline-pumping action as you navigate through a variety of challenging levels, each filled with obstacles and enemies. With each stage presenting new challenges, you'll need to hone your archery skills to overcome the odds and emerge triumphant.
  • What makes Stickman Archer 3 stand out is its intuitive controls and realistic physics, which provide a satisfying shooting experience. Adjust your aim, factor in distance and wind, and release your arrow with precision to eliminate your enemies with deadly accuracy.
  • Challenge yourself to conquer the campaign mode, where you'll battle through increasingly difficult levels filled with waves of enemies. Or test your skills in endless mode, where the challenges never cease, and your accuracy will be put to the ultimate test.
  • Immerse yourself in the addictive gameplay of Stickman Archer 3 and prove that you have what it takes to be the ultimate stickman archer. With its simple yet challenging mechanics, this game will keep you entertained for hours as you strive for mastery.

How to Play

  • Tap and hold to aim your bow.
  • Release to shoot your arrow.
  • Adjust your aim to account for distance and wind.
  • Defeat all enemies to progress through the levels.

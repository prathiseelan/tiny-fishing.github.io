Welcome to Stickman Parkour

  • Embark on an adrenaline-fueled adventure in Stickman Parkour, where you'll guide a fearless stickman through a series of challenging obstacles and death-defying jumps. Prepare yourself for an intense parkour experience that will test your reflexes and agility!
  • Experience the thrill of extreme platforming as you navigate through dynamic levels filled with obstacles and hazards. Jump, slide, and climb your way through each level, using your parkour skills to overcome every obstacle in your path.
  • What sets Stickman Parkour apart is its fast-paced gameplay and fluid controls, allowing you to perform a variety of parkour moves with precision and ease. From wall jumps to flips, you'll have the freedom to unleash your inner parkour master and conquer each level.
  • Challenge yourself to complete each level in the fastest time possible, competing against friends and players from around the world to climb the leaderboards and prove your parkour prowess. With its addictive gameplay and endless replay value, Stickman Parkour will keep you coming back for more!

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your stickman.
  • Press the spacebar to jump, and use the arrow keys to perform various parkour moves.
  • Navigate through obstacles and hazards to reach the end of each level.
  • Complete each level as quickly as possible to earn a high score and climb the leaderboards.

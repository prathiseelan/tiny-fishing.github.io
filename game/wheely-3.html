Welcome to Wheely 3

Join Wheely on another thrilling adventure in Wheely 3 as he sets out to solve puzzles, overcome obstacles, and unravel mysteries in a charming and whimsical world. With its captivating storyline, engaging puzzles, and delightful characters, Wheely 3 promises to take players on an unforgettable journey filled with excitement and fun.

In this exciting installment of the series, Wheely finds himself embarking on a quest to rescue his beloved car friend, who has been kidnapped by mysterious foes. Navigate through a series of captivating levels filled with clever puzzles and challenging obstacles as you help Wheely track down his friend and unravel the secrets behind their disappearance.

Wheely 3 features a diverse range of puzzles and challenges, each requiring players to use logic, creativity, and problem-solving skills to progress. From navigating treacherous landscapes to outsmarting cunning enemies, Wheely 3 offers a rich and rewarding gameplay experience that will keep players engaged for hours on end.

With its charming visuals, intuitive controls, and heartwarming storyline, Wheely 3 is suitable for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a casual gamer looking for a fun and engaging experience, Wheely 3 offers something for everyone.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to interact with objects and navigate Wheely through each level.

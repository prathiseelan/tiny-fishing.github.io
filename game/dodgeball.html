How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to throw the ball.
  • Avoid incoming balls by moving quickly and using obstacles for cover.
  • Catch balls thrown by opponents to eliminate them from the game.

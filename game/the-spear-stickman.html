Welcome to The Spear Stickman

  • Prepare for an epic battle of precision and skill in The Spear Stickman, where you'll embark on a quest to defeat your enemies with well-aimed spear throws. Step into the shoes of a courageous stickman warrior and prove your worth on the battlefield!
  • Experience the thrill of intense combat as you hone your throwing skills and take down waves of enemy stickmen. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, The Spear Stickman offers hours of entertainment for players of all skill levels.
  • What sets The Spear Stickman apart is its realistic physics and challenging levels. Test your aim and timing as you launch spears at moving targets, adjusting your angle and power to score direct hits. With each successful throw, you'll inch closer to victory!
  • Challenge yourself to conquer increasingly difficult levels and defeat powerful bosses as you progress through the game. With multiple weapons and upgrades to unlock, there's always a new challenge waiting for you in The Spear Stickman.
  • Immerse yourself in the adrenaline-pumping action of The Spear Stickman and prove yourself as the ultimate stickman warrior. With its engaging gameplay and thrilling combat mechanics, this game will keep you on the edge of your seat!

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to aim and adjust the angle of your throw.
  • Click and hold to set the power of your throw, then release to launch the spear.
  • Aim for the head or body of your enemies to score direct hits and defeat them.
  • Collect coins and power-ups to unlock new weapons and upgrades.

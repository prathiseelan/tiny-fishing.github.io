Welcome to Basketball Line

  • Get ready to test your skills in Basketball Line, an addictive and challenging game where you draw lines to guide basketballs into the hoop. Aim, draw, and score as many baskets as you can in this captivating arcade experience.
  • Use your finger or mouse to draw lines on the screen, directing the basketballs toward the hoop. With each successful shot, your score increases, but beware of obstacles and moving platforms that can obstruct your path.
  • What sets Basketball Line apart is its simple yet engaging gameplay, offering endless fun and excitement for players of all ages. With its intuitive controls and vibrant graphics, it's easy to pick up and play, but challenging to master.
  • Challenge yourself through multiple levels with increasing difficulty, each presenting new obstacles and opportunities to showcase your accuracy and precision.
  • Immerse yourself in the addictive gameplay of Basketball Line. Whether you're aiming for the high score or just looking for a fun way to pass the time, this game promises hours of entertainment and enjoyment.

How to Play

  • Use your finger or mouse to draw lines on the screen.
  • Guide the basketballs into the hoop by drawing paths for them to follow.
  • Avoid obstacles and moving platforms that may block the basketball's path.
  • Score as many baskets as possible to increase your points and advance through levels.
  • Challenge yourself to beat your high score and become a master of Basketball Line!

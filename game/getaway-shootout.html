Welcome to Getaway Shootout

  • Prepare for a wild and chaotic multiplayer experience in Getaway Shootout, where you'll engage in intense battles against friends or AI opponents. Jump, shoot, and outmaneuver your rivals as you strive to be the last one standing!
  • Experience the adrenaline rush of fast-paced platforming action as you navigate through a variety of challenging levels. Use your agility and quick reflexes to dodge obstacles, leap across platforms, and grab powerful weapons to gain the upper hand in battle.
  • What sets Getaway Shootout apart is its unique blend of platforming and shooting gameplay. With physics-based mechanics and interactive environments, every match offers unpredictable and hilarious moments that keep you coming back for more.
  • Challenge yourself in solo mode as you battle against increasingly difficult AI opponents, or gather your friends for epic multiplayer showdowns. Whether you're competing for bragging rights or just looking to have a good time, Getaway Shootout delivers non-stop action and excitement!

Key Features:

  • Fast-paced multiplayer platforming action for up to four players.
  • Dynamic physics-based gameplay with interactive environments.
  • A variety of weapons and power-ups to discover and utilize.
  • Challenging single-player mode with progressively tougher AI opponents.
  • Multiple themed levels with unique obstacles and hazards.

Welcome to Soccar

  • Step onto the pitch and experience the adrenaline-fueled action of Soccar, a thrilling combination of soccer and car racing. Strap into your vehicle, rev your engines, and prepare for high-octane matches where speed, skill, and strategy collide!
  • Engage in heart-pounding matches where cars serve as the players and the ball is your ticket to victory. Work with your teammates to outmaneuver the opposition, pass the ball, and score epic goals while fending off the opposing team's attacks.
  • What sets Soccar apart is its dynamic gameplay and physics-driven car mechanics. Master the art of drifting, boost your way to victory, and unleash powerful shots to outplay your opponents and secure victory on the pitch.
  • Challenge yourself in a variety of game modes, from intense 1v1 duels to chaotic multiplayer matches. Whether you're playing solo or teaming up with friends, Soccar offers endless excitement and competitive gameplay for players of all skill levels.
  • Immerse yourself in the fast-paced world of Soccar and compete against players from around the globe in thrilling online matches. Climb the ranks, earn rewards, and prove your skills as the ultimate Soccar champion!

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to boost your speed.
  • Hit the ball with your car to pass or shoot.
  • Work with your teammates to coordinate plays and score goals.

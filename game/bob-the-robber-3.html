Welcome to Bob The Robber 3

  • Guide Bob through a series of challenging levels set in various environments, including high-security buildings, ancient temples, and futuristic facilities. Use your stealth skills to avoid detection, disable alarms, and solve puzzles to progress.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe gestures to move Bob.
  • Avoid security cameras, guards, and traps to stay undetected.
  • Disable security systems and unlock doors to progress through each level.
  • Collect loot and valuable items to increase your score.
  • Complete bonus objectives to earn extra rewards.

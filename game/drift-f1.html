How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your F1 car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to initiate a drift.
  • Adjust your speed and angle to maintain control during drifts.
  • Upgrade your car's performance and customize its appearance in the garage.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl to accelerate and the brake.
  • Avoid obstacles and navigate the desert terrain to reach the finish line.
  • Upgrade your car and unlock new vehicles as you progress through the game.

Welcome to Puppet Master

  • Step into the enchanting world of Puppet Master, where you wield the power of magic strings to control a cast of whimsical puppets. Embark on an epic adventure filled with mystery, danger, and intrigue as you unravel the secrets of the puppet realm!
  • As the Puppet Master, you have the ability to manipulate your puppet companions using your magical strings. Guide them through treacherous obstacles, solve challenging puzzles, and overcome formidable enemies as you journey across fantastical landscapes.
  • What sets Puppet Master apart is its unique blend of platforming action and puzzle-solving elements. Each puppet possesses special abilities that you must master to progress through the game. From the nimble acrobat to the mighty warrior, each puppet offers a distinct playstyle and contributes to the adventure in its own way.
  • Challenge yourself to unravel the mysteries of the puppet realm as you traverse through beautifully crafted levels filled with hidden secrets and surprises. Collect magical artifacts, unlock new puppets, and uncover the truth behind the ancient prophecy that threatens to engulf the world in darkness.
  • Immerse yourself in the captivating story of Puppet Master and experience a journey like no other. With its charming characters, stunning visuals, and engaging gameplay, this game will transport you to a world of wonder and magic!

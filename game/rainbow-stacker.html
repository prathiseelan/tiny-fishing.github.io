Welcome to Rainbow Stacker

  • Experience the mesmerizing challenge of Rainbow Stacker, a delightful puzzle game that tests your precision and timing. Stack colorful blocks as high as you can while trying to keep them balanced and aligned.
  • Immerse yourself in a world of vibrant hues and soothing music as you embark on a journey to create the tallest rainbow tower. With each perfectly placed block, your tower will grow higher, but beware – one wrong move could send it toppling to the ground!
  • What sets Rainbow Stacker apart is its simple yet addictive gameplay. Tap the screen at just the right moment to drop each block onto the tower below. The closer you get to the center, the more stable your tower will be, but timing is key!
  • Challenge yourself to beat your high score and reach new heights with each playthrough. As you progress, the game becomes increasingly challenging, requiring split-second timing and flawless precision to keep your tower from collapsing.
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Rainbow Stacker and test your stacking skills against players from around the world. With its colorful visuals, addictive gameplay, and endless replay value, this game is sure to keep you entertained for hours on end!

Key Features:

  • Addictive puzzle gameplay that challenges your precision and timing.
  • Vibrant graphics and soothing music for a relaxing gaming experience.
  • Simple controls – just tap the screen to drop each block.
  • Endless replay value with progressively challenging levels.
  • Global leaderboards to compete with friends and players worldwide.

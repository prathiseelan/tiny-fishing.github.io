Welcome to Wheely 7

Get ready for another thrilling adventure with Wheely in Wheely 7, the latest installment of the beloved puzzle series. Join Wheely as he embarks on a journey to save his friends and uncover the secrets of a mysterious alien planet. With its captivating storyline, challenging puzzles, and charming characters, Wheely 7 promises an unforgettable gaming experience that will keep players of all ages entertained for hours.

In this exciting chapter of the series, Wheely finds himself stranded on a strange alien world after crash-landing his spaceship. Help Wheely navigate through the alien landscape by solving a series of increasingly difficult puzzles, overcoming obstacles, and outsmarting clever aliens. With each new challenge, Wheely gets closer to rescuing his friends and unraveling the mysteries of the alien planet.

Wheely 7 features a wide range of puzzles and challenges set across different environments on the alien planet. From exploring mysterious caves to navigating high-tech facilities, Wheely 7 offers a diverse and engaging gameplay experience that will test players' logic, problem-solving skills, and creativity.

With its vibrant graphics, intuitive controls, and delightful storyline, Wheely 7 is suitable for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a casual gamer looking for a fun and relaxing experience, Wheely 7 offers something for everyone to enjoy.

Key Features

  • Exciting storyline set on a mysterious alien planet.
  • Dozens of challenging puzzles and obstacles to overcome.
  • Colorful and imaginative environments inspired by alien landscapes.
  • Quirky characters and humorous dialogue to enjoy throughout the journey.
  • Hours of fun and entertainment for players of all ages.

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to interact with objects and navigate Wheely through each level.
  • Click on items in the environment to trigger actions and solve puzzles.
  • Help Wheely overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and rescue his friends.
  • Collect stars and unlock bonus levels for additional challenges and rewards.

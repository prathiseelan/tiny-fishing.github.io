Welcome to Doodle Jump

  • Embark on an endless jumping adventure in Doodle Jump, where you'll guide a cute character through a series of platforms, obstacles, and challenges. Prepare to jump your way to new heights!
  • Experience the addictive gameplay of Doodle Jump as you bounce your way up the screen, avoiding monsters and collecting power-ups along the way. With its simple controls and charming graphics, Doodle Jump offers endless hours of fun.
  • What sets Doodle Jump apart is its procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two games are ever the same. With each playthrough, you'll encounter new challenges and opportunities to reach higher scores.
  • Challenge yourself to climb as high as possible while avoiding obstacles and defeating enemies. With its fast-paced gameplay and intuitive controls, Doodle Jump is easy to pick up but difficult to master.
  • Immerse yourself in the whimsical world of Doodle Jump and compete against friends and players from around the world to see who can reach the highest score. With its addictive gameplay and endless replay value, this game will keep you coming back for more.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or tilt your device to move your character left or right.
  • Press the screen to jump and bounce off platforms.
  • Avoid falling off the screen or getting caught by enemies.
  • Collect power-ups and coins to boost your score and abilities.

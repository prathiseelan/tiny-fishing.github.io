Welcome to Cannon Basketball

  • Experience the excitement of basketball with a twist in Cannon Basketball, a thrilling physics-based sports game that challenges your shooting skills. Get ready to aim, launch, and score baskets using a powerful cannon!
  • Immerse yourself in the fast-paced and addictive gameplay of Cannon Basketball as you navigate through a series of challenging levels filled with obstacles, moving platforms, and tricky puzzles. With intuitive controls and dynamic level design, Cannon Basketball offers an engaging and entertaining gaming experience.
  • What makes Cannon Basketball stand out is its unique blend of basketball and puzzle elements. Strategize your shots and utilize the environment to your advantage as you aim to sink baskets from various angles and distances.
  • Challenge yourself in the single-player mode, where you'll tackle increasingly difficult levels and obstacles. With multiple game modes and customizable cannons, Cannon Basketball provides endless opportunities for creative gameplay and skillful shots.
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant visuals and energetic soundtrack of Cannon Basketball, creating an immersive and enjoyable gaming atmosphere. With its addictive gameplay and challenging levels, Cannon Basketball promises hours of entertainment.

How to Play

  • Adjust the angle and power of your cannon using the mouse or touchscreen.
  • Aim carefully and shoot the basketball into the hoop to score points.
  • Utilize the environment to your advantage, bouncing basketballs off walls and obstacles to reach challenging baskets.
  • Complete each level by successfully scoring all the baskets within the given number of shots.
  • Unlock new levels, cannons, and achievements as you progress through the game and hone your skills.

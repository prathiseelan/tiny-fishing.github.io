Welcome to Crossy Road

  • Embark on a thrilling journey across busy streets, treacherous rivers, and perilous landscapes in Crossy Road, the addictive arcade game that puts your reflexes to the test. Guide your character through an endless maze of obstacles and see how far you can go!
  • Experience the excitement and challenge of crossing roads, rivers, and train tracks as you navigate through a pixelated world filled with obstacles and hazards. With its charming retro graphics and simple yet addictive gameplay, Crossy Road offers hours of endless fun for players of all ages.
  • What sets Crossy Road apart is its endless gameplay and wide variety of characters to unlock. From chickens and frogs to unicorns and dinosaurs, each character brings its own unique abilities and challenges, adding depth and replay value to the game.
  • Challenge yourself to beat your high score in the single-player mode, or compete against friends and players from around the world in multiplayer challenges. With its intuitive controls and addictive gameplay, Crossy Road is perfect for quick gaming sessions or long-lasting fun.
  • Immerse yourself in the fast-paced excitement of Crossy Road and see how far you can go. Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick thrill or a dedicated gamer aiming for the top of the leaderboards, this game offers something for everyone.

How to Play

  • Tap or click on the screen to move your character forward.
  • Avoid oncoming traffic, jump over rivers, and dodge obstacles to progress.
  • Collect coins and power-ups to unlock new characters and enhance your abilities.
  • Test your reflexes and see how far you can go in this endless arcade adventure!

How to Play

  • Use the mouse to aim and adjust the power of your golf swing.
  • Click to hit the golf ball and send it soaring through the underwater course.
  • Avoid obstacles, navigate hazards, and aim for the hole to complete each level.
  • Collect power-ups and bonus items to enhance your golfing experience.
  • Compete with friends and players worldwide to see who can achieve the lowest score!
